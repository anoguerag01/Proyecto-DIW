EduMapa Presentation

Created by: Alba Noguera | Released: 2026

View full video transcript (Accessibility)

Have you ever lost your notes right before the exam or missed class and no one gives you what they covered?

Don't worry, because that's why I created this application. It's a web platform designed by and for students where our goal is to make life much easier. It's the ultimate place to upload, organize, and find all the notes you need to pass.

The idea is to create a great collaborative community. Here you can find notes on absolutely everything. Do you need a visual summary to understand the French Revolution? We have it. Looking for biology outlines or web programming tricks? They are here too.

You just have to use the search engine, filter by subject, and in a couple of clicks, you'll have on your screen the best materials shared by your own classmates.

And the best of all is how easy it is to do your part. Uploading your own notes takes seconds. Also, the application is fully responsive and accessible. What does this mean? Well, you can take a picture of your notebook and upload it directly from your mobile while riding the bus.

The design is thought out with clear colors and fonts so you can read for hours without straining your eyes. But this isn't just a drawer to store PDFs, it's a community.

That's why we have a chat section where you can ask questions live, debate a syllabus, or even form study groups.

Ultimately, this is here so we can all help each other. Join, share your knowledge, find what you're missing, and go for that A+. See you inside.
